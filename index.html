Welcome To Ventura's Earth Sciences’ website.

Ventura Earth Sciences specializes in Environmental Forensics. Property owners, attorneys, lenders, regulatory agencies, and other stakeholders often disagree over the responsibility for cleaning up subsurface contamination. The primary goal of environmental forensics is to develop an understanding of the nature and history of the release, who has been impacted, whether other sources exist, and what processes may be affecting the concentration trends (temporally and migration distance-wise).

SERVICES:

• Use of forensics and isotopic analysis to identify and differentiate sources of contamination to ensure that responsibility for the remediation is equitable;
• Use of stable isotopes to focus remediation effort and minimize the carbon footprint consistent with green remediation concepts; and,
• Use of stable isotopes to determine efficacy of treatment.

Ventura Earth Scinces provides services from designing sampling programs that are typically phased to minimize sampling redundancy while maintaining appropriate quality assurance/quality control requirements, to directing that the laboratory analysis includes corroborating parameters and methodologies to withstand scrutiny, and using this data to provide scientifically-defensible interpretations.
